# Roots Education Hub: CEO + CTO Execution Plan

## 1) Vision and Strategic Intent

## Positioning
Roots Education Hub is the operating system for student growth across schools, tuition centres, and paramedical colleges.

## North Star
Every learner gets a personalized daily growth path.

## Promises
- Product promise: learn better, practice smarter, choose careers earlier.
- Brand promise: trusted, local, affordable, AI-guided education at global quality.
- Outcome promise: measurable improvements in scores, skills, and career readiness.

## 2) Ecosystem Product Blueprint

## Product Surfaces
- Student App (`Classteacher`): daily hub, AI tutor, exam prep, skill tracks, career pathways.
- Teacher Hub: class analytics, intervention suggestions, assignment and assessment workflows.
- Institution Console: enrollment, performance, governance, communication.
- Parent Companion (Phase 2+): visibility, alerts, recommended actions, fee/payment rails.

## Shared Platform Layer
- Unified identity and role model (student, teacher, parent, admin).
- Unified learner graph (academics, habits, interests, goals).
- Shared design system and component registry.
- Event and analytics pipeline for product + learning outcomes.

## 3) 12-Month Execution Roadmap

## Phase 1 (Day 0 to Day 90): MVP Hardening + Pilot Outcomes
- Design system v1 live across student app.
- Classteacher core loops production-ready.
- Pilot with 5-10 institutions.
- Baseline analytics and outcome instrumentation.

## Phase 2 (Month 3 to Month 6): AI Personalization + Teacher Value
- AI personalization v1 for student practice paths.
- Teacher intervention workflow.
- Parent visibility v1.
- Retention and engagement loops (daily challenge, leaderboard, streaks).

## Phase 3 (Month 6 to Month 9): Multi-Institution Scale + Pathways
- Multi-institution controls and governance.
- Paramedical and tuition-specific pathways.
- Career mentoring modules and recommendations.

## Phase 4 (Month 9 to Month 12): Global Readiness
- Localization stack and curriculum variants.
- Partner APIs and integration marketplace.
- Global launch narrative with validated impact proof points.

## 4) Operating Model

## Product Squads
- Student Experience Squad
- Teacher Success Squad
- Institution Platform Squad
- AI + Data Platform Squad
- Growth + Partnerships Squad

## Cadence
- Weekly release trains.
- Monthly CEO/CTO product and metrics review.
- Quarterly strategy reset and investment reallocation.

## Quality Gates (Required before production release)
- UX QA and accessibility check
- Pedagogy review
- AI safety and bias review
- Security and privacy review

## 5) Architecture and Reliability Commitments

## Core Stack
- Next.js frontends (student, teacher, admin surfaces).
- Scalable API layer with domain services.
- Event-driven data pipeline and observability.

## AI Stack
- Recommendation engine.
- Doubt-solving assistant.
- Adaptive practice generator.
- Early risk alerts for intervention.

## Safety and Compliance
- Child-safe defaults and moderated responses.
- Role-based access and audit trails.
- Encryption in transit and at rest.
- Consent and data retention controls.

## SLO Targets
- 99.9% uptime target.
- Mobile-first performance budgets on low-mid devices.
- Offline-friendly flows for core student actions.

## 6) KPI Framework

## Learning Impact
- Score lift by subject and cohort.
- Mastery progression and completion rates.
- Time-to-mastery reduction.

## Engagement
- DAU/WAU and week-4 retention.
- Daily challenge participation.
- Streak quality (not just streak length).

## Teacher and Institution Value
- Planning/evaluation time saved.
- Intervention effectiveness.
- Institution retention and satisfaction.

## Business Health
- CAC/LTV ratio.
- Net revenue retention.
- Referral and partner-led growth.
